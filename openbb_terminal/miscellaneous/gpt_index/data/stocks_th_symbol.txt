parent_command:stocks/th
usage: symbol [-n SYMBOL]

Select the symbol you wish to trade

optional arguments:
  -n SYMBOL, --name SYMBOL
                        Symbol (default: None)


Examples:
- To trade a specific stock, use: stocks/th/symbol -n <SYMBOL>
- Select the stock you want to trade with: stocks/th/symbol --name <SYMBOL>
- Choose the stock for trading by entering its symbol: stocks/th/symbol -n <SYMBOL>
