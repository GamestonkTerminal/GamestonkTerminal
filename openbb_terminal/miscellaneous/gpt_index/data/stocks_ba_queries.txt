parent_command:stocks/ba
usage: queries [-l LIMIT]

Print top related queries with this stock's query. [Source: Google]

optional arguments:
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        limit of top related queries to print. (default: 10)


Examples:
- To display the top 10 related queries for a stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ba/queries
- To display the top 5 related queries for a stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ba/queries -l 5
- For a specific stock, show 7 top related queries: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ba/queries --limit 7
- Retrieve the top 3 related queries for a stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ba/queries -l 3
- Display the top 8 related queries for a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ba/queries --limit 8
