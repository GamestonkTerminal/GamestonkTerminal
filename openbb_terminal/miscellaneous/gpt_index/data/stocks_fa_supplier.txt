parent_command:stocks/fa
usage: supplier [-t TICKER]

List of suppliers from ticker provided. [Source: CSIMarket]

optional arguments:
  -t TICKER, --ticker TICKER
                        Ticker to analyze


Examples:
- To obtain a list of suppliers for a specific stock, use: stocks/fa/supplier -t <SYMBOL>
- For a list of suppliers related to a given stock, try: stocks/fa/supplier --ticker <SYMBOL>
- To analyze the suppliers of a particular stock, enter: stocks/fa/supplier -t <SYMBOL>
- Retrieve the list of suppliers associated with a specific stock by using: stocks/fa/supplier --ticker <SYMBOL>
