parent_command:stocks/ca
usage: balance [-q] [-t S_TIMEFRAME]

Prints either yearly or quarterly balance statement the company, and compares it against similar companies.

optional arguments:
  -q, --quarter         Quarter financial data flag. (default: False)
  -t S_TIMEFRAME, --timeframe S_TIMEFRAME
                        Specify yearly/quarterly timeframe. Default is last. (default: None)


Examples:
- To display the yearly balance statement for a company: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/balance
- To display the quarterly balance statement for a company: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/balance -q
- To display the balance statement for a specific quarter: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/balance -t Q3
- To display the balance statement for a specific year: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/balance -t 2021
- Show the yearly balance statement and compare with similar companies: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/balance
- Show the quarterly balance statement and compare with similar companies: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/balance -q
- Compare the balance statement of a company for a specific quarter with its peers: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/balance -t Q3
- Compare the balance statement of a company for a specific year with its peers: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/balance -t 2021
