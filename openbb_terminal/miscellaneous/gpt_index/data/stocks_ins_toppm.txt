parent_command:stocks/ins
usage: toppm [-l LIMIT]

Print top officer purchases of the month. [Source: OpenInsider]

optional arguments:
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        Limit of datarows to display (default: 10)


Examples:
- To view the top officer purchases of the month for all stocks: stocks/ins/toppm
- To display the top 5 officer purchases of the month: stocks/ins/toppm -l 5
- If you want to see the top 20 officer purchases this month: stocks/ins/toppm --limit 20
