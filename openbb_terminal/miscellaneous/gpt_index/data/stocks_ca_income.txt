parent_command:stocks/ca
usage: income [-q] [-t S_TIMEFRAME]

Prints either yearly or quarterly income statement the company, and compares it against similar companies.

optional arguments:
  -q, --quarter         Quarter financial data flag. (default: False)
  -t S_TIMEFRAME, --timeframe S_TIMEFRAME
                        Specify yearly/quarterly timeframe. Default is last. (default: None)


Examples:
- To view the yearly income statement for a specific company: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/income
- To view the quarterly income statement for a specific company: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/income -q
- To view the income statement for a specific company with a specified timeframe: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/income -t 2021Q3
- Display the quarterly income statement for the last quarter of a specific company: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/income --quarter --timeframe last
