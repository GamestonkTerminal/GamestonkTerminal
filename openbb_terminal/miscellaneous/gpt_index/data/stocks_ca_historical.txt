parent_command:stocks/ca
usage: historical [-t {o,h,l,c,a}] [-n] [-s START] [-e END]

Historical price comparison between similar companies.

optional arguments:
  -t {o,h,l,c,a}, --type {o,h,l,c,a}
                        Candle data to use: o-open, h-high, l-low, c-close, a-adjusted close. (default: a)
  -n, --normalize       Flag to normalize all prices on same 0-1 scale (default: False)
  -s START, --start START
                        The starting date (format YYYY-MM-DD) of the stocks (default: 2022-06-01)
  -e END, --end END     The end date (format YYYY-MM-DD) of the stocks (default: 2023-06-02)


Examples:
- Compare historical adjusted close prices of similar companies: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/historical
- Compare historical open prices of similar companies: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/historical -t o
- Compare historical low prices of similar companies with normalized values: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/historical -t l -n
- Compare historical high prices of similar companies between specified dates: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/historical -t h -s 2022-01-01 -e 2022-12-31
- Compare historical close prices of similar companies with normalization and specific date range: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/historical -t c -n -s 2021-01-01 -e 2021-12-31
