parent_command:stocks/disc
usage: gainers [-l LIMIT]

Print up to 25 top gainers. [Source: Yahoo Finance]

optional arguments:
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        Limit of stocks to display. (default: 5)


Examples:
- To display the top 5 gainers: stocks/disc/gainers
- To display the top 10 gainers: stocks/disc/gainers -l 10
- To display the top 25 gainers: stocks/disc/gainers --limit 25
