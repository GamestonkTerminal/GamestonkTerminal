parent_command:stocks/dps
usage: dpotc

Display barchart of dark pool (ATS) and OTC (Non ATS) data. [Source: FINRA]

dpotc
Examples:
- To display a barchart of dark pool and OTC data for a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/dps/dpotc
- Show dark pool and OTC barchart for a stock by loading its symbol: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/dps/dpotc
- Visualize dark pool and OTC data for a stock using a barchart: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/dps/dpotc
- Load a stock symbol and display its dark pool and OTC data in a barchart: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/dps/dpotc
- Access dark pool and OTC data barchart for a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/dps/dpotc
