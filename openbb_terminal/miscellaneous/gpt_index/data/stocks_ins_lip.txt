parent_command:stocks/ins
usage: lip [-l LIMIT]

Print latest insider purchases. [Source: OpenInsider]

optional arguments:
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        Limit of datarows to display (default: 10)


Examples:
- To view the latest insider purchases for <SYMBOL>: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/lip
- To display the latest 5 insider purchases for a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/lip -l 5
- To show the 20 most recent insider purchases for <SYMBOL>: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/lip --limit 20
