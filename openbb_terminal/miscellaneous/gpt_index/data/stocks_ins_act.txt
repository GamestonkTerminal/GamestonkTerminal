parent_command:stocks/ins
usage: act [--raw]

Prints insider activity over time [Source: Business Insider]

optional arguments:
  --raw  Print raw data.


Examples:
- To display insider activity for a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/act
- To display raw insider activity data for a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/act --raw
- Show insider activity over time for a stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/act
- View raw data of insider activity for a particular stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/act --raw
- Retrieve insider activity information for a stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/act
- Access raw data on insider activity for a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/act --raw
