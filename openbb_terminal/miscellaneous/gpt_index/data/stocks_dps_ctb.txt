parent_command:stocks/dps
usage: ctb [-n NUMBER] [--raw]

Show cost to borrow of stocks. [Source: Stocksera/Interactive Broker]

optional arguments:
  -n NUMBER, --number NUMBER
                        Number of records to retrieve. (default: 20)
  --raw                 Print raw data. (default: False)


Examples:
- To display the cost to borrow for the top 20 stocks: stocks/dps/ctb
- To show the cost to borrow for a specific number of stocks, for example, 10: stocks/dps/ctb -n 10
- To view the cost to borrow for the top 20 stocks and print raw data: stocks/dps/ctb --raw
- To show the cost to borrow for 15 stocks and display raw data: stocks/dps/ctb -n 15 --raw
