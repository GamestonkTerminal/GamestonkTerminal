parent_command:stocks/gov
usage: lobbying [-l LIMIT]

Lobbying details [Source: www.quiverquant.com]

optional arguments:
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        Limit of events to show (default: 10)


Examples:
- To display the top 10 lobbying events for a specific stock, use: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/gov/lobbying
- For displaying the top 5 lobbying events related to a stock, try: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/gov/lobbying -l 5
- To see the top 20 lobbying events for a particular stock, enter: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/gov/lobbying --limit 20
