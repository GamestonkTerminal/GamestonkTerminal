parent_command:stocks/dps
usage: shorted [-l LIMIT]

Print up to 25 top ticker most shorted. [Source: Yahoo Finance]

optional arguments:
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        Limit of the most shorted stocks to retrieve. (default: 10)


Examples:
- To display the top 10 most shorted stocks: stocks/dps/shorted
- To show the top 5 most shorted stocks: stocks/dps/shorted -l 5
- If you want to view the top 20 most shorted stocks: stocks/dps/shorted --limit 20
