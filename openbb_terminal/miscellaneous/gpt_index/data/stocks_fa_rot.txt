usage: rot [-t TICKER] [-l LIMIT] [--raw]

Rating over time (monthly). [Source: Finnhub]

optional arguments:
  -t TICKER, --ticker TICKER
                        Ticker to analyze
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        Limit of last months
  --raw                 Only output raw data


Examples:
- To analyze the rating over time for a specific stock symbol, use: stocks/fa/rot -t <SYMBOL>
- To analyze the rating over time for a stock symbol and limit the results to the last 6 months: stocks/fa/rot -t <SYMBOL> -l 6
- To analyze the rating over time for a stock symbol and display raw data: stocks/fa/rot -t <SYMBOL> --raw
- If you want to analyze the rating over time for a specific stock symbol and limit the results to the last 3 months: stocks/fa/rot -t <SYMBOL> -l 3
