parent_command:stocks/ca
usage: overview

Prints screener data of similar companies. [Source: Finviz]

overview
Examples:
- Get an overview of similar companies for a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/overview
- Display screener data for companies similar to the loaded stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/overview
- Find and display similar companies' screener data for the given stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/overview
- Load a stock symbol and show an overview of related companies: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/overview
- Obtain an overview of companies similar to the chosen stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/overview
