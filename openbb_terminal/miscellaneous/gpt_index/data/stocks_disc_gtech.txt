parent_command:stocks/disc
usage: gtech [-l LIMIT]

Print up to 25 top tech stocks with revenue and earnings growth in excess of 25%. [Source: Yahoo Finance]

optional arguments:
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        Limit of stocks to display. (default: 5)


Examples:
- To display the top 5 tech stocks with high revenue and earnings growth: stocks/disc/gtech
- To display the top 10 tech stocks with high revenue and earnings growth: stocks/disc/gtech -l 10
- To display the top 15 tech stocks with high revenue and earnings growth: stocks/disc/gtech --limit 15
