parent_command:stocks/ca
usage: get [-u] [-n] [-l LIMIT]

Get similar companies from selected data source (default: Finviz) to compare with.

optional arguments:
  -u, --us_only         Show only stocks from the US stock exchanges. Works only with Polygon (default: False)
  -n, --nocountry       Similar stocks from finviz using only Industry and Sector. (default: False)
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        Limit of stocks to retrieve. (default: 10)


Examples:
- To retrieve 10 similar companies for comparison: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/get
- To get 5 similar companies for comparison: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/get -l 5
- To display similar US companies only: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/get -u
- To find similar stocks without considering the country: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/get -n
- To get 7 similar US companies for comparison: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/get -u -l 7
- To display 6 similar stocks without considering the country: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/get -n -l 6
