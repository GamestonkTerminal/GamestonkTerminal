parent_command:stocks/options
usage: greeks [-d DIVIDEND] [-r RISK_FREE] [-p] [-m MIN] [-M MAX] [-a]

The greeks for a given option.

optional arguments:
  -d DIVIDEND, --div DIVIDEND
                        The dividend continuous rate (default: 0)
  -r RISK_FREE, --risk-free RISK_FREE
                        The risk free rate (default: None)
  -p, --put             Whether the option is a put. (default: False)
  -m MIN, --min MIN     Minimum strike price to show. (default: None)
  -M MAX, --max MAX     Maximum strike price to show. (default: None)
  -a, --all             Whether to show all greeks. (default: False)


Examples:
- To calculate the greeks for all options of a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/options/greeks
- Calculate the greeks for put options only: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/options/greeks -p
- Show greeks for options with a minimum strike price of 30: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/options/greeks -m 30
- Display greeks for options with a maximum strike price of 100: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/options/greeks -M 100
- Calculate greeks for options with strike prices between 50 and 150: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/options/greeks -m 50 -M 150
- Show all greeks for a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/options/greeks -a
- Calculate greeks for options with a dividend continuous rate of 0.02: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/options/greeks -d 0.02
- Display greeks for options with a risk-free rate of 0.03: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/options/greeks -r 0.03
- Calculate greeks for put options with a minimum strike price of 20 and a maximum strike price of 80: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/options/greeks -p -m 20 -M 80
- Show greeks for all options with a dividend continuous rate of 0.01 and a risk-free rate of 0.02: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/options/greeks -d 0.01 -r 0.02
