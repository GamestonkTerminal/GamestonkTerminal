parent_command:stocks/ins
usage: lins [-l LIMIT]

Prints information about inside traders. The following fields are expected: Date, Relationship, Transaction, #Shares, Cost, Value ($), #Shares Total, Insider Trading, SEC Form 4. [Source: Finviz]

optional arguments:
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        Limit of latest inside traders.


Examples:
- To display information about inside traders for a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/lins
- To show information on inside traders with a limit of 5 records: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/lins -l 5
- For viewing the latest 10 inside traders for a stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/lins --limit 10
