parent_command:stocks/ca
usage: rmv [-s L_SIMILAR]

Remove similar tickers to compare with.

optional arguments:
  -s L_SIMILAR, --similar L_SIMILAR
                        Tickers to remove from similar list (default: [])


Examples:
- Remove a specific similar ticker for comparison: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/rmv -s AAPL
- Eliminate multiple similar tickers from the comparison list: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/rmv -s AAPL MSFT GOOG
- Clear the list of similar tickers for comparison: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/rmv --similar
- Exclude a single similar ticker from the comparison: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/rmv --similar TSLA
- Remove several tickers from the list of similar ones: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/rmv -s NFLX AMZN FB
