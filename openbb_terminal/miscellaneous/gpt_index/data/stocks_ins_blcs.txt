parent_command:stocks/ins
usage: blcs [-l LIMIT]

Print latest CEO/CFO sales 100k. [Source: OpenInsider]

optional arguments:
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        Limit of datarows to display (default: 10)


Examples:
- To view the latest CEO/CFO sales 100k for a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/blcs
- Display the latest 5 CEO/CFO sales 100k for a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/blcs -l 5
- Show the latest CEO/CFO sales 100k for a specific stock with a limit of 20 rows: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/blcs --limit 20
- Retrieve the latest 15 CEO/CFO sales 100k for a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/blcs -l 15
