parent_command:stocks/options
usage: eodchain [-d DATE]

Get full option chain at a given end of day.

optional arguments:
  -d DATE, --date DATE  Date to get option chain for. Format YYYY-MM-DD (default: None)


Examples:
- To retrieve the full option chain for a specific end of day, use: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/options/eodchain
- If you want to obtain the full option chain for a specific date, use: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/options/eodchain -d 2022-12-31
- To view the option chain for a certain end of day for a specific stock, execute: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/options/eodchain
- In order to fetch the option chain at the end of day for a given date, run: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/options/eodchain --date 2022-12-31
