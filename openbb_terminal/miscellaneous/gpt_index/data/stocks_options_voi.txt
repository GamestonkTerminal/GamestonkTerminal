           [-e {2023-06-02,2023-06-09,2023-06-16,2023-06-23,2023-06-30,2023-07-07,2023-07-21,2023-08-18,2023-09-15,2023-10-20,2023-11-17,2023-12-15,2024-01-19,2024-03-15,2024-06-21,2024-09-20,2024-12-20,2025-01-17,2025-06-20,2025-12-19,}]

Plots Volume + Open Interest of calls vs puts.

optional arguments:
  -v MIN_VOL, --minv MIN_VOL
                        minimum volume (considering open interest) threshold of the plot. (default: -1)
  -m MIN_SP, --min MIN_SP
                        minimum strike price to consider in the plot. (default: -1)
  -M MAX_SP, --max MAX_SP
                        maximum strike price to consider in the plot. (default: -1)
  -e {2023-06-02,2023-06-09,2023-06-16,2023-06-23,2023-06-30,2023-07-07,2023-07-21,2023-08-18,2023-09-15,2023-10-20,2023-11-17,2023-12-15,2024-01-19,2024-03-15,2024-06-21,2024-09-20,2024-12-20,2025-01-17,2025-06-20,2025-12-19,}, --expiration {2023-06-02,2023-06-09,2023-06-16,2023-06-23,2023-06-30,2023-07-07,2023-07-21,2023-08-18,2023-09-15,2023-10-20,2023-11-17,2023-12-15,2024-01-19,2024-03-15,2024-06-21,2024-09-20,2024-12-20,2025-01-17,2025-06-20,2025-12-19,}
                        Select expiration date (YYYY-MM-DD) (default: )


Examples:
- To plot Volume + Open Interest of calls vs puts for a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/options/voi
- To display Volume + Open Interest for a stock with a minimum volume threshold of 100: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/options/voi -v 100
- To visualize Volume + Open Interest for a stock with a strike price range between 20 and 80: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/options/voi -m 20 -M 80
- To plot Volume + Open Interest for a stock with options expiring on a specific date: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/options/voi -e 2023-06-30
- To display Volume + Open Interest for a stock with a minimum volume threshold of 200 and a strike price range between 30 and 90: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/options/voi -v 200 -m 30 -M 90
- To visualize Volume + Open Interest for a stock with a minimum volume threshold of 300 and options expiring on a specific date: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/options/voi -v 300 -e 2023-07-07
- To plot Volume + Open Interest for a stock with a strike price range between 40 and 100 and options expiring on a specific date: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/options/voi -m 40 -M 100 -e 2023-08-18
- To display Volume + Open Interest for a stock with a minimum volume threshold of 400, a strike price range between 50 and 110, and options expiring on a specific date: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/options/voi -v 400 -m 50 -M 110 -e 2024-01-19
