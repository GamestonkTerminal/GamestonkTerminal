parent_command:stocks/disc
usage: asc [-l LIMIT]

Print up to 25 small cap stocks with earnings growth rates better than 25%. [Source: Yahoo Finance]

optional arguments:
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        Limit of stocks to display. (default: 5)


Examples:
- To display the top 5 small cap stocks with earnings growth rates better than 25%: stocks/disc/asc
- To display the top 10 small cap stocks with earnings growth rates better than 25%: stocks/disc/asc -l 10
- For showing 15 small cap stocks with earnings growth rates over 25%: stocks/disc/asc --limit 15
