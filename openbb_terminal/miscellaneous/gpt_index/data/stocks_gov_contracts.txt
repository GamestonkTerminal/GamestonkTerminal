parent_command:stocks/gov
usage: contracts [-p PAST_TRANSACTION_DAYS] [--raw]

Contracts associated with ticker. [Source: www.quiverquant.com]

optional arguments:
  -p PAST_TRANSACTION_DAYS, --past_transaction_days PAST_TRANSACTION_DAYS
                        Past transaction days (default: 10)
  --raw                 Print raw data. (default: False)


Examples:
- To view contracts associated with a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/gov/contracts
- To view contracts for a specific stock within the past 30 days: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/gov/contracts -p 30
- To view contracts for a specific stock and display raw data: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/gov/contracts --raw
- Show contracts for a stock within the past 5 days: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/gov/contracts -p 5
