parent_command:stocks/ins
usage: tist [-l LIMIT]

Print top insider sales of the day. [Source: OpenInsider]

optional arguments:
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        Limit of datarows to display (default: 10)


Examples:
- To view the top insider sales of the day: stocks/ins/tist
- To display the top 5 insider sales of the day: stocks/ins/tist -l 5
- To show the top 20 insider sales today: stocks/ins/tist --limit 20
