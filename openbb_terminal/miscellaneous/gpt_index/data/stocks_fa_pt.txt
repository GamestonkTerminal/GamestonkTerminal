parent_command:stocks/fa
usage: pt [-t TICKER]

Prints price target from analysts. [Source: Business Insider and Financial Modeling Prep]

optional arguments:
  -t TICKER, --ticker TICKER
                        Ticker to analyze


Examples:
- To view the price target for a specific stock, use: stocks/fa/pt -t <SYMBOL>
- Display the analyst price target for a stock by entering: stocks/fa/pt --ticker <SYMBOL>
- Obtain the price target from analysts for a particular stock with: stocks/fa/pt -t <SYMBOL>
- Check the analyst's price target for a given stock using: stocks/fa/pt --ticker <SYMBOL>
