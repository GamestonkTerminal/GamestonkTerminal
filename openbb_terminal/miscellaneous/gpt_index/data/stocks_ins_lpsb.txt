parent_command:stocks/ins
usage: lpsb [-l LIMIT]

Print latest penny stock buys. [Source: OpenInsider]

optional arguments:
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        Limit of datarows to display (default: 10)


Examples:
- To display the latest penny stock buys: stocks/ins/lpsb
- To display the top 5 latest penny stock buys: stocks/ins/lpsb -l 5
- To show the latest 20 penny stock buys: stocks/ins/lpsb --limit 20
