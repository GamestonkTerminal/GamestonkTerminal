parent_command:stocks/disc
usage: fipo [-d DAYS] [-s END] [-l LIMIT]

Future IPOs dates. [Source: https://finnhub.io]

optional arguments:
  -d DAYS, --days DAYS  Number of days in the future to look for IPOs. (default: 5)
  -s END, --end END     The end date (format YYYY-MM-DD) to look for IPOs, starting from today. When set, end date will override --days argument (default: None)
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        Limit number of IPOs to display. (default: 20)


Examples:
- To view future IPOs in the next 5 days: stocks/disc/fipo
- To view future IPOs in the next 10 days: stocks/disc/fipo -d 10
- To view future IPOs until a specific end date: stocks/disc/fipo -s 2023-01-31
- To limit the number of displayed future IPOs to 10: stocks/disc/fipo -l 10
- To view future IPOs in the next 7 days and limit the display to 15: stocks/disc/fipo -d 7 -l 15
- To view future IPOs until a specific end date and limit the display to 5: stocks/disc/fipo -s 2023-02-28 -l 5
