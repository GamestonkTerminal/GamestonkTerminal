parent_command:stocks/ins
usage: blip [-l LIMIT]

Print latest insider purchases 25k. [Source: OpenInsider]

optional arguments:
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        Limit of datarows to display (default: 10)


Examples:
- View the latest insider purchases for any stock: stocks/ins/blip
- Display the top 5 insider purchases: stocks/ins/blip -l 5
- Show the latest 20 insider purchases with a higher limit: stocks/ins/blip --limit 20
