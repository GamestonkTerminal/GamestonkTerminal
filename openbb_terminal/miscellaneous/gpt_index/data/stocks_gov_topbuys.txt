parent_command:stocks/gov
usage: topbuys [-g {congress,senate,house}] [-p PAST_TRANSACTIONS_MONTHS] [-l LIMIT] [--raw]

Top buys for government trading. [Source: www.quiverquant.com]

optional arguments:
  -g {congress,senate,house}, --govtype {congress,senate,house}
  -p PAST_TRANSACTIONS_MONTHS, --past_transactions_months PAST_TRANSACTIONS_MONTHS
                        Past transaction months (default: 6)
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        Limit of top tickers to display (default: 10)
  --raw                 Print raw data. (default: False)


Examples:
- To view the top 10 stock buys for all of Congress in the past 6 months: stocks/gov/topbuys
- To display the top 5 stock buys for the Senate in the past 3 months: stocks/gov/topbuys -g senate -p 3 -l 5
- To see the top 20 stock buys for the House of Representatives in the past 12 months: stocks/gov/topbuys -g house -p 12 -l 20
- To get the top 15 stock buys for the entire Congress in the past 2 months and display raw data: stocks/gov/topbuys -p 2 -l 15 --raw
