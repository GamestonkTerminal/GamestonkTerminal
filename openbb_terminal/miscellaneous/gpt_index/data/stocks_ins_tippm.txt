parent_command:stocks/ins
usage: tippm [-l LIMIT]

Print top insider purchases of the month. [Source: OpenInsider]

optional arguments:
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        Limit of datarows to display (default: 10)


Examples:
- To view the top 10 insider purchases of the month: stocks/ins/tippm
- Display the top 5 insider purchases for the month: stocks/ins/tippm -l 5
- Show the top 20 monthly insider purchases: stocks/ins/tippm --limit 20
- Retrieve the top 15 insider purchases this month: stocks/ins/tippm -l 15
- Get the top 8 insider purchases for the current month: stocks/ins/tippm --limit 8
