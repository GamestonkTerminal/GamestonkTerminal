parent_command:stocks/ins
usage: blop [-l LIMIT]

Print latest officer purchases 25k. [Source: OpenInsider]

optional arguments:
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        Limit of datarows to display (default: 10)


Examples:
- To display the latest officer purchases for a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/blop
- To display the latest officer purchases for a specific stock with a custom limit: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/blop -l 20
- Display the latest officer purchases for a specific stock with a limit of 5: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/blop --limit 5
