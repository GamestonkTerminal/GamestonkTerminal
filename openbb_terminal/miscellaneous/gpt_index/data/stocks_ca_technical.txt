parent_command:stocks/ca
usage: technical

Prints screener data of similar companies. [Source: Finviz]

technical
Examples:
- To display technical screener data for similar companies of a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/technical
- For viewing technical screener data of companies similar to a particular stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/technical
- To get technical data of similar companies for a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/technical
- Retrieve technical screener data for companies similar to a given stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/technical
