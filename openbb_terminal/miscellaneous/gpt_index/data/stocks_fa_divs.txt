parent_command:stocks/fa
usage: divs [-t TICKER] [-l LIMIT] [-p]

Historical dividends for a company

optional arguments:
  -t TICKER, --ticker TICKER
                        Ticker to analyze (default: None)
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        Number of previous dividends to show (default: 16)
  -p, --plot            Plots changes in dividend over time (default: False)


Examples:
- To see the historical dividends for a specific company: stocks/fa/divs -t <SYMBOL>
- To analyze the historical dividends for a company with a specific limit: stocks/fa/divs -t <SYMBOL> -l 10
- To visualize the changes in dividend over time for a specific company: stocks/fa/divs -t <SYMBOL> -p
- To display the historical dividends for a company and plot the changes over time: stocks/fa/divs -t <SYMBOL> -p -l 20
- To examine the last 5 dividends for a company: stocks/fa/divs -t <SYMBOL> -l 5
