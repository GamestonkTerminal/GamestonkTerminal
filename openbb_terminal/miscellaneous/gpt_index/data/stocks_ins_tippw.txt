parent_command:stocks/ins
usage: tippw [-l LIMIT]

Print top insider purchases of the week. [Source: OpenInsider]

optional arguments:
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        Limit of datarows to display (default: 10)


Examples:
- To view the top insider purchases of the week for all stocks: stocks/ins/tippw
- To display the top 5 insider purchases of the week: stocks/ins/tippw -l 5
- If you want to see the top 20 insider purchases this week: stocks/ins/tippw --limit 20
