parent_command:stocks
usage: news [-t TICKER] [-d N_START_DATE] [-o] [-s SOURCES]

latest news of the company

optional arguments:
  -t TICKER, --ticker TICKER
                        Ticker to get data for
  -d N_START_DATE, --date N_START_DATE
                        The starting date (format YYYY-MM-DD) to search articles from
  -o, --oldest          Show oldest articles first
  -s SOURCES, --sources SOURCES
                        Show news only from the sources specified (e.g bloomberg,reuters)


Examples:
- To view the latest news for a specific company: stocks/news -t <SYMBOL>
- To see news articles for a company starting from a specific date: stocks/news -t <SYMBOL> -d 2022-01-01
- To display the oldest news articles first for a company: stocks/news -t <SYMBOL> -o
- To show news from specific sources for a company: stocks/news -t <SYMBOL> -s bloomberg,reuters
- To view news for a company starting from a specific date and showing the oldest articles first: stocks/news -t <SYMBOL> -d 2022-01-01 -o
- To see news articles for a specific company from a specific source starting from a particular date: stocks/news -t <SYMBOL> -d 2022-01-01 -s bloomberg
