parent_command:stocks/ca
usage: sentiment_compare [-r]

FinBrain's sentiment comparison across similar tickers.

optional arguments:
  -r, --raw  Display raw sentiment data (default: False)


Examples:
- To view a sentiment comparison across similar tickers for a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/sentiment_compare
- To display raw sentiment data for a sentiment comparison across similar tickers: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/sentiment_compare -r
