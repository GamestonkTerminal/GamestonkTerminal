parent_command:stocks/ins
usage: blis [-l LIMIT]

Print latest insider sales 100k. [Source: OpenInsider]

optional arguments:
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        Limit of datarows to display (default: 10)


Examples:
- To display the latest insider sales for a stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/blis
- Show the 5 most recent insider sales for a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/blis -l 5
- Retrieve the latest 20 insider sales for a particular stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/blis --limit 20
