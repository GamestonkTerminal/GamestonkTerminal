parent_command:stocks/ca
usage: performance

Prints screener data of similar companies. [Source: Finviz]

performance
Examples:
- Retrieve performance data for similar companies to a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/performance
- Compare the performance of similar companies to a given stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/performance
- Get screener data for companies related to a particular stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/performance
- Analyze the performance of companies similar to a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/performance
- Examine the performance of companies in the same sector as a chosen stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ca/performance
