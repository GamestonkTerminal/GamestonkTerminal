parent_command:stocks/disc
usage: ulc [-l LIMIT]

Print up to 25 potentially undervalued large cap stocks. [Source: Yahoo Finance]

optional arguments:
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        Limit of stocks to display. (default: 5)


Examples:
- To display the top 5 potentially undervalued large cap stocks: stocks/disc/ulc
- To display the top 10 potentially undervalued large cap stocks: stocks/disc/ulc -l 10
- If you want to see 15 potentially undervalued large cap stocks: stocks/disc/ulc --limit 15
