parent_command:stocks/ins
usage: stats [-l LIMIT] [-u]

Open insider filtered data using selected ticker. [Source: OpenInsider]

optional arguments:
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        Limit of datarows to display (default: 10)
  -u, --urls            Flag to show hyperlinks (default: False)


Examples:
- To display insider filtered data for a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/stats
- To show insider filtered data with a limit of 5 rows: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/stats -l 5
- To display insider filtered data and include hyperlinks: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/stats -u
- To show insider filtered data with a limit of 20 rows and include hyperlinks: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/stats -l 20 -u
