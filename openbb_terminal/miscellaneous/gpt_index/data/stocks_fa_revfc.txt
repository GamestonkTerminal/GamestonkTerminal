parent_command:stocks/fa
usage: revfc [-t TICKER]

Estimated revenue [Source: Seeking Alpha]

optional arguments:
  -t TICKER, --ticker TICKER
                        Ticker to analyze (default: None)


Examples:
- To analyze the estimated revenue for a specific stock: stocks/fa/revfc -t <SYMBOL>
- Analyze the estimated revenue of a company using its ticker symbol: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/fa/revfc
- Estimate the revenue for a stock with its ticker: stocks/fa/revfc --ticker <SYMBOL>
