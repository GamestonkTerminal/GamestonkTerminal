parent_command:stocks/fa
usage: analysis [-t TICKER]

Display analysis of SEC filings based on NLP model. [Source: https://eclect.us]

optional arguments:
  -t TICKER, --ticker TICKER
                        Ticker to analyze (default: None)


Examples:
- Analyze SEC filings for a specific stock: stocks/fa/analysis -t <SYMBOL>
- To analyze the filings of a particular company, use: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/fa/analysis
- Display analysis of SEC filings for the desired stock: stocks/fa/analysis --ticker <SYMBOL>
- For SEC filings analysis of a specific stock, run: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/fa/analysis
