parent_command:stocks/disc
usage: ugs [-l LIMIT]

Print up to 25 undervalued stocks with revenue and earnings growth in excess of 25%. [Source: Yahoo Finance]

optional arguments:
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        Limit of stocks to display. (default: 5)


Examples:
- To display 5 undervalued stocks with high revenue and earnings growth: stocks/disc/ugs
- To display 10 undervalued stocks with significant growth: stocks/disc/ugs -l 10
- To show 15 undervalued stocks with growth over 25%: stocks/disc/ugs --limit 15
