parent_command:stocks/disc
usage: trending [-i N_ID] [-l LIMIT] [-d S_DATE]

Trending news articles. [Source: Seeking Alpha]

optional arguments:
  -i N_ID, --id N_ID    article ID (default: -1)
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        limit of articles being printed (default: 5)
  -d S_DATE, --date S_DATE
                        starting date of articles (default: 2023-06-02)


Examples:
- Retrieve trending news articles for stocks: stocks/disc/trending
- Obtain the top 10 trending news articles: stocks/disc/trending -l 10
- Show trending news articles starting from a specific date: stocks/disc/trending -d 2023-07-01
- Display a specific article by its ID: stocks/disc/trending -i 1234
- Fetch the top 3 trending news articles since a particular date: stocks/disc/trending -l 3 -d 2023-07-15
- Access a specific article and limit the number of displayed articles: stocks/disc/trending -i 5678 -l 5
