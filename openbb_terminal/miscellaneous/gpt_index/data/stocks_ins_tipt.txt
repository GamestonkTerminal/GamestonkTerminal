parent_command:stocks/ins
usage: tipt [-l LIMIT]

Print top insider purchases of the day. [Source: OpenInsider]

optional arguments:
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        Limit of datarows to display (default: 10)


Examples:
- To view the top insider purchases of the day: stocks/ins/tipt
- To display the top 5 insider purchases of the day: stocks/ins/tipt -l 5
- To show the top 20 insider purchases of the day: stocks/ins/tipt --limit 20
