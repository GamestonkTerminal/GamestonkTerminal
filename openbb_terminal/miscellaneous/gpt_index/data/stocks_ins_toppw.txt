parent_command:stocks/ins
usage: toppw [-l LIMIT]

Print top officer purchases of the week. [Source: OpenInsider]

optional arguments:
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        Limit of datarows to display (default: 10)


Examples:
- To display the top 10 officer purchases of the week: stocks/ins/toppw
- To display the top 5 officer purchases of the week: stocks/ins/toppw -l 5
- If you want to see the top 20 officer purchases of the week: stocks/ins/toppw --limit 20
