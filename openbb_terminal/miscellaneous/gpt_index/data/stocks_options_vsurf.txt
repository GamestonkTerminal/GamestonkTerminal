usage: vsurf [-z {IV,OI,LP}]

Plot 3D volatility surface.

optional arguments:
  -z {IV,OI,LP}, --z-axis {IV,OI,LP}
                        The data for the Z axis (default: IV)


Examples:
- To plot a 3D volatility surface with implied volatility for <SYMBOL>: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/options/vsurf
- For visualizing a 3D volatility surface with open interest as the Z axis for a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/options/vsurf -z OI
- To display a 3D volatility surface using the last price data for the Z axis for <SYMBOL>: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/options/vsurf -z LP
