parent_command:stocks/fa
usage: epsfc [-t TICKER]

Estimated EPS [Source: Seeking Alpha]

optional arguments:
  -t TICKER, --ticker TICKER
                        Ticker to analyze (default: None)


Examples:
- To view the estimated EPS for a specific stock: stocks/fa/epsfc -t <SYMBOL>
- Analyze the estimated EPS of a company using its ticker: stocks/fa/epsfc --ticker <SYMBOL>
- Estimate the EPS for a stock by providing its ticker: stocks/fa/epsfc -t <SYMBOL>
