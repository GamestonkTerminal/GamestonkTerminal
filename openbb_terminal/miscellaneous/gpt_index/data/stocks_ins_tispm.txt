parent_command:stocks/ins
usage: tispm [-l LIMIT]

Print top insider sales of the month. [Source: OpenInsider]

optional arguments:
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        Limit of datarows to display (default: 10)


Examples:
- To view the top 10 insider sales of the month: stocks/ins/tispm
- To display the top 5 insider sales of the month: stocks/ins/tispm -l 5
- If you want to see the top 20 insider sales for the month: stocks/ins/tispm --limit 20
