parent_command:stocks/options/screen
usage: view [-p {high_iv,v_below_20day,liquid_otm,spy_30_delta,template}]

View available presets under presets folder.

optional arguments:
  -p {high_iv,v_below_20day,liquid_otm,spy_30_delta,template}, --preset {high_iv,v_below_20day,liquid_otm,spy_30_delta,template}
                        View specific custom preset


Examples:
- To view all available presets: stocks/options/screen/view
- To view the high_iv preset: stocks/options/screen/view -p high_iv
- To view the v_below_20day preset: stocks/options/screen/view -p v_below_20day
- To view the liquid_otm preset: stocks/options/screen/view -p liquid_otm
- To view the spy_30_delta preset: stocks/options/screen/view -p spy_30_delta
- To view the template preset: stocks/options/screen/view -p template
