parent_command:stocks/dps
usage: psi [--nyse]

Shows price vs short interest volume. [Source: Quandl/Stockgrid]

optional arguments:
  --nyse  Data from NYSE flag. Otherwise comes from NASDAQ. Only works for Quandl.


Examples:
- To display price vs short interest volume for a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/dps/psi
- To show price vs short interest volume for a stock using NYSE data: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/dps/psi --nyse
- For NASDAQ data on price vs short interest volume of a stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/dps/psi
