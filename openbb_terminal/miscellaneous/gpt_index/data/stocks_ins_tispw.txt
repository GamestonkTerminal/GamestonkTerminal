parent_command:stocks/ins
usage: tispw [-l LIMIT]

Print top insider sales of the week. [Source: OpenInsider]

optional arguments:
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        Limit of datarows to display (default: 10)


Examples:
- Display the top insider sales of the week for all stocks: stocks/ins/tispw
- Show the top 5 insider sales of the week: stocks/ins/tispw -l 5
- Retrieve the top 20 insider sales of the week: stocks/ins/tispw --limit 20
