parent_command:stocks/ins
usage: lis [-l LIMIT]

Print latest insider sales. [Source: OpenInsider]

optional arguments:
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        Limit of datarows to display (default: 10)


Examples:
- To display the latest insider sales for a stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/lis
- To show the latest 5 insider sales for a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/lis -l 5
- For the latest 20 insider sales of a particular stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/lis --limit 20
- To view the most recent insider sales for a stock, limited to 15 records: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/lis -l 15
