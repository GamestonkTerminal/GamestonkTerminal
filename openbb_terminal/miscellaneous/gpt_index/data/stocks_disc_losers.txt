parent_command:stocks/disc
usage: losers [-l LIMIT]

Print up to 25 top losers. [Source: Yahoo Finance]

optional arguments:
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        Limit of stocks to display. (default: 5)


Examples:
- To display the top 5 losers in the stock market: stocks/disc/losers
- To view the top 10 losers in the stock market: stocks/disc/losers -l 10
- For a list of the top 15 losing stocks: stocks/disc/losers --limit 15
