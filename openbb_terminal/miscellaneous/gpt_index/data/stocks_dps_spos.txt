parent_command:stocks/dps
usage: spos [-n NUM] [-r]

Shows Net Short Vol. vs Position. [Source: Stockgrid]

optional arguments:
  -n NUM, --number NUM  Number of last open market days to show
  -r, --raw             Flag to print raw data instead


Examples:
- To display net short volume vs position for a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/dps/spos
- Show net short volume vs position for the last 10 open market days: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/dps/spos -n 10
- Display net short volume vs position for a stock and print raw data: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/dps/spos -r
- View net short volume vs position for the last 5 open market days with raw data: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/dps/spos -n 5 -r
