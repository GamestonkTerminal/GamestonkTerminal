parent_command:stocks/ba
usage: regions [-l LIMIT]

Plot bars of regions based on stock's interest. [Source: Google]

optional arguments:
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        limit of regions to plot that show highest interest. (default: 10)


Examples:
- To plot bars of regions based on a stock's interest: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ba/regions
- For displaying the top 5 regions with the highest interest in a stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ba/regions -l 5
- To visualize the top 7 regions with the highest interest for a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ba/regions --limit 7
- Show the top 3 regions with the highest interest in a stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ba/regions -l 3
- To display the top 10 regions with the highest interest for a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ba/regions --limit 10
