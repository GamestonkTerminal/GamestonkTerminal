parent_command:stocks/disc
usage: hotpenny [-l LIMIT]

Provides top penny stocks from various websites. [Source: Yfinance]

optional arguments:
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        limit of stocks to display (default: 5)


Examples:
- To display the top 5 penny stocks: stocks/disc/hotpenny
- To display the top 10 penny stocks: stocks/disc/hotpenny -l 10
- For a list of the top 20 penny stocks: stocks/disc/hotpenny --limit 20
