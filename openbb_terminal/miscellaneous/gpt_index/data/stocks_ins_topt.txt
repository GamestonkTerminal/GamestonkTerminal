parent_command:stocks/ins
usage: topt [-l LIMIT]

Print top officer purchases of the day. [Source: OpenInsider]

optional arguments:
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        Limit of datarows to display (default: 10)


Examples:
- To view the top 10 officer purchases of the day: stocks/ins/topt
- To display the top 5 officer purchases of the day: stocks/ins/topt -l 5
- To see the top 20 officer purchases of the day: stocks/ins/topt --limit 20
