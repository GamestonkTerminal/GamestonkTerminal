parent_command:stocks/fa
usage: earnings [-t TICKER] [-q] [-l LIMIT]

Print earnings dates and reported EPS of the company. The following fields are expected: Fiscal Date Ending and Reported EPS. [Source: Alpha Vantage]

optional arguments:
  -t TICKER, --ticker TICKER
                        Ticker to analyze (default: None)
  -q, --quarter         Quarter fundamental data flag. (default: False)
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        Number of latest info (default: 5)


Examples:
- To view the earnings dates and reported EPS of a company: stocks/fa/earnings -t <SYMBOL>
- To analyze the earnings and EPS for a specific ticker: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/fa/earnings
- To display the latest 10 earnings dates and reported EPS: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/fa/earnings -l 10
- For quarterly fundamental data of a company: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/fa/earnings -q
- To check the earnings and EPS of a ticker with a limit of 3 latest entries: stocks/fa/earnings -t <SYMBOL> -l 3
- To view the quarterly earnings and EPS for a specific company with a limit of 7: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/fa/earnings -q -l 7
