parent_command:stocks
usage: quote [-t S_TICKER]

Current quote for the loaded stock ticker.

optional arguments:
  -t S_TICKER, --ticker S_TICKER
                        Get a quote for a specific ticker, or comma-separated list of tickers. (default: AAPL)


Examples:
- Retrieve the current quote for the loaded stock ticker: stocks/quote
- Get a quote for a specific stock ticker: stocks/quote -t <SYMBOL>
- Obtain quotes for multiple stock tickers separated by commas: stocks/quote -t <SYMBOL1>,<SYMBOL2>,<SYMBOL3>
