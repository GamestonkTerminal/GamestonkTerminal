parent_command:stocks/ins
usage: filter [-l LIMIT] [-u]

Print open insider filtered data using loaded preset. [Source: OpenInsider]

optional arguments:
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        Limit of datarows to display (default: 10)
  -u, --urls            Flag to show hyperlinks (default: False)


Examples:
- To display the top 10 open insider transactions for a stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/filter
- Show the top 20 open insider transactions for a stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/filter -l 20
- Display the top 5 open insider transactions with hyperlinks: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/filter -l 5 -u
- Retrieve open insider transactions for a specific stock and include hyperlinks: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/filter -u
- For a particular stock, display open insider filtered data with a limit of 15 rows: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/filter -l 15
