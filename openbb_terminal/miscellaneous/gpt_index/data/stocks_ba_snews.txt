parent_command:stocks/ba
usage: snews

Display stock price and headlines sentiment using VADER model over time. [Source: Finnhub]

snews
Examples:
- To display stock price and headlines sentiment for a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ba/snews
- Show the sentiment of news headlines and stock price for a company: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ba/snews
- Analyze the sentiment of stock price and news headlines over time for a particular stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ba/snews
- View the stock price and news sentiment using VADER model for a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ba/snews
