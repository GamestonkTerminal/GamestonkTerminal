parent_command:stocks/ins
usage: blcp [-l LIMIT]

Print latest CEO/CFO purchases 25k. [Source: OpenInsider]

optional arguments:
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        Limit of datarows to display (default: 10)


Examples:
- To display the latest CEO/CFO purchases for a stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/blcp
- To display the latest CEO/CFO purchases for a stock with a specific limit: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/blcp -l 5
- Show the latest 7 CEO/CFO purchases for a stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/blcp --limit 7
- Retrieve the 15 most recent CEO/CFO purchases for a stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/blcp -l 15
