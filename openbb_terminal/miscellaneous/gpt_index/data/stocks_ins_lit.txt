parent_command:stocks/ins
usage: lit [-l LIMIT]

Print latest insider trading. [Source: OpenInsider]

optional arguments:
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        Limit of datarows to display (default: 10)


Examples:
- To display the latest insider trading for a stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/lit
- To display the latest insider trading with a limit of 5 rows: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/lit -l 5
- Show the latest 15 insider trades for a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ins/lit --limit 15
