parent_command:stocks/fa
usage: customer [-t TICKER]

List of customers from ticker provided. [Source: CSIMarket]

optional arguments:
  -t TICKER, --ticker TICKER
                        Ticker to analyze


Examples:
- To view the list of customers for a specific stock, use: stocks/fa/customer -t <SYMBOL>
- Get the customers of a particular stock by entering its ticker: stocks/fa/customer --ticker <SYMBOL>
