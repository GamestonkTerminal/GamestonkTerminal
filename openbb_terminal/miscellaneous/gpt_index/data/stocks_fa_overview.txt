parent_command:stocks/fa
usage: overview [-t TICKER]

Prints information about, among other things, the industry, sector exchange and company description.

optional arguments:
  -t TICKER, --ticker TICKER
                        Ticker to analyze (default: None)


Examples:
- To get an overview of a specific stock: stocks/fa/overview -t <SYMBOL>
- Display information about a stock, including its industry, sector, and exchange: stocks/fa/overview --ticker <SYMBOL>
- Analyze a company's description and other details by entering its ticker: stocks/fa/overview -t <SYMBOL>
