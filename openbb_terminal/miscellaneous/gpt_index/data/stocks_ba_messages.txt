parent_command:stocks/ba
usage: messages [-l LIMIT]

Print up to 30 of the last messages on the board. [Source: Stocktwits]

optional arguments:
  -l LIMIT, --limit LIMIT
                        limit messages shown. (default: 30)


Examples:
- To display the last 30 messages on the board for <SYMBOL>: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ba/messages
- To show the 10 most recent messages on the board for <SYMBOL>: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ba/messages -l 10
- To view the last 5 messages on the board for a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/ba/messages --limit 5
