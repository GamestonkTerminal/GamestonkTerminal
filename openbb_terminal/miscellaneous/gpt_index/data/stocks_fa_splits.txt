parent_command:stocks/fa
usage: splits [-t TICKER]

Stock splits and reverse split events since IPO [Source: Yahoo Finance]

optional arguments:
  -t TICKER, --ticker TICKER
                        Ticker to analyze (default: None)


Examples:
- To view stock splits and reverse split events for a specific ticker: stocks/fa/splits -t <SYMBOL>
- Analyzing stock splits and reverse splits for a given ticker: stocks/fa/splits --ticker <SYMBOL>
- Retrieve stock split history for a particular stock: stocks/fa/splits -t <SYMBOL>
- Display stock splits and reverse splits since IPO for a specific stock: stocks/fa/splits --ticker <SYMBOL>
